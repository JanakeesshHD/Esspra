Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

2022

Company Founded

Esspra Industries Private Limited was established in Karnataka, India, with a focus on developing innovative induction cooking solutions.

2022

First Product Launch

Launched our first line of residential induction cooktops, setting new standards for energy efficiency and design.

2022

Commercial Division

Expanded into commercial induction solutions, serving restaurants, hotels, and catering businesses.

2023

Industrial Applications

Developed specialized induction heating systems for industrial applications, broadening our product portfolio.

2023

Manufacturing Expansion

Expanded our manufacturing facilities to meet growing demand for our induction products and solutions.
