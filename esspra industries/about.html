About Esspra Industries

Who We Are

Pioneering Induction Technology for a Better Future

Esspra Industries Private Limited (EIPL) is a registered startup operating as a Private Limited Indian Non-Government Company incorporated in India on 13 September 2022 (Two years and seven months 10 days old ). Its registered office is in Bangaluru, Karnataka, India.Esspra Industries Pvt Ltd is a leading manufacturer of induction stoves and induction solutions.
