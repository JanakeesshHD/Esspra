Installation & Setup

Installation Requirements

  • The cooktop must be installed by a qualified electrician in accordance with local electrical codes.
  • Requires a dedicated 32A circuit with proper grounding.
  • Minimum 30mm clearance required between the bottom of the cooktop and any surface below.
  • Minimum 50mm clearance required from the edges of the cut-out to any adjacent walls.
  • Ensure proper ventilation with at least 5mm clearance around the cooktop.
  • The countertop material must be heat-resistant and at least 30mm thick.

Installation Process

  1. Prepare the countertop cut-out according to the specified dimensions (560mm × 490mm).
  2. Apply the included sealing tape around the bottom edge of the cooktop.
  3. Connect the power cable to the appropriate electrical supply (must be done by a qualified electrician).
  4. Carefully place the cooktop into the cut-out, ensuring it is centered.
  5. Secure the cooktop using the mounting brackets provided.
  6. Test all cooking zones to ensure proper operation.

Initial Setup

  1. Remove all protective packaging and stickers from the cooktop surface.
  2. Clean the glass surface with a suitable glass cleaner before first use.
  3. Power on the cooktop and follow the on-screen instructions to set language preferences and time.
  4. To connect to Wi-Fi, download the Esspra Smart Kitchen app and follow the in-app instructions to pair your cooktop.
  5. Perform a test run of each cooking zone to ensure proper functionality.
